What's IAYPAA?

Iowa Young People In Alcoholics Anonymous provides an opportunity for A.A. members from all over the state to come together and share their experience, strength, and hope.

It is a function of A.A. members in accordance with GSO guidelines for 'Conference and Conventions' that allows for heavy participation in service aspects of the conference by newer and/or younger Iowa members, providing exposure to the 12 Traditions, 12 Concepts for World Service, service committees, and a convention of A.A. members—all vital components of the attraction that keep people coming back.

IAYPAA conference was first held in Des Moines, Iowa in 2016. It continues to move to different cities across the state. At each conference, bids are accepted from cities wishing to host the next IAYPAA conference.
